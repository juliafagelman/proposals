Customer Community Program Launch

Client: Guidde

Project Overview & Objectives

A well-designed customer community doesn’t just connect users, it scales customer success, drives product adoption, and turns customers into advocates.

This project involves designing, building, and launching a branded community hub that aligns with Guidde’s business objectives. Deliverables will include a ready-to-launch community platform, initial content creation, governance documentation, and a strategy for sustainable management.

Key goals for this community:

  • Enhance Customer Engagement: Create a space for customers to share experiences, inspire one another, and celebrate wins.
  • Build Brand Loyalty: A professional, on-brand hub that fosters a sense of belonging and creates advocates.
  • Centralize Product Feedback: Help product teams prioritize features that matter most, transforming feedback into a roadmap advantage.

Project Phases & Deliverables

Phase 1: Discovery & Strategic Alignment

Define purpose, success metrics, and stakeholder alignment.

  • Kickoff & goal-setting workshops.
  • Identify community objectives, target audiences, and key success metrics.
  • Internal stakeholder mapping and alignment.

Deliverable: Discovery summary & strategy brief.

Phase 2: Platform Implementation

Choose and implement the right platform.

  • Select the right community platform (e.g., Circle, Discourse).
  • Configure platform structure, permissions, and branding.
  • Set up user onboarding flows and moderation roles.

Deliverable: Live, branded community environment & admin documentation.

Phase 3: Community Launch & Content Activation

Prepare and develop initial content; engage first users.

  • Create 10–15 initial discussion posts, tutorials, and welcome content.
  • Invite and onboard internal champions and external early adopters.
  • Establish and publish initial community guidelines.

Deliverable: Engaged early-access community with foundational starter content.

Phase 4: Governance & Enablement

Define rules, moderation, and management strategy.

  • Write and finalize official Rules of Engagement.
  • Develop a comprehensive management playbook (moderation, reporting, feedback loops).
  • Train internal champions on community management best practices.

Deliverable: “Community Management Playbook” & recorded walkthrough.

Phase 5: Sustainability & Handoff

Ensure sustainability post-launch.

  • Review initial community performance against success metrics.
  • Finalize all documentation and make final edits.
  • Conduct official wrap-up and handoff meeting with the Guidde team.

Deliverable: Final project closeout package and recommendations.

Proposed Timeline

Month Key Milestones

Month 2 Platform configuration; initial content creation; internal champion alignment

All phases are flexible to accommodate Guidde’s internal approval cycles and technical dependencies.

Success Metrics

  • Branded community platform launched and fully functional.
  • 10–15 pieces of initial content live and engaging members.
  • First cohort of active community members onboarded and participating.
  • Operational workflows and governance processes documented.
  • Clear ownership and handoff to Guidde’s internal community lead.
