Action Plan

Phase 1: Product Training & Discovery (2 days)

  • Define Critical Information: Define what is the most important information for early adopters to successfully get started with Handshaik.
  • Understand User Profiles: Better understanding of users who will be consuming documentation (e.g. are they Admins, Workflow builders, End users).
  • Select & Prepare Tools: Access provided to live environment and other tools (e.g. Notion).
  • Product Immersion: Product training sessions to be run by Handshaik to ensure thorough understanding of the platform's current capabilities, limitations, popular actions/use cases and specific terminology to be used consistently across all documentation.

Phase 2: Content Planning (1 day)

  • Article Structure Definition: Define a comprehensive list of articles to be produced.
  • Identify Core Use Cases: Brainstorm and select the most popular and straightforward customer use cases. These examples will be woven into articles, where there is a necessity to provide more contextual explanation (if at all).
  • Content Prioritization: Determine priority and timeline for article creation, ensuring that the most critical content is developed first to unblock early users.

Phase 3: Content Creation (4 days)

  • Initial Article Production: Delivery of two initial articles.
  • Initial Feedback Cycle: Approval of style and content on two initial articles.
  • Article Production: Remaining articles produced in the order of priority determined in Phase 2 and with feedback taken on board.
  • Images/Gifs: Adding images/gifs to all articles.
  • Establish Feedback Mechanism: Create a form to collect feedback on articles from customers.

Phase 4: Delivery and Iteration (1-3 days depending on feedback)

  • Delivery: Articles delivered for review.
  • Iterative Feedback Loop: Feedback on content's accuracy, clarity, and tone.
  • Edits: Any changes or missing information incorporated.
  • Final Delivery: Final versions of all articles delivered, ready for use.
  • Next Steps: Following trial.

Next Steps (Post-Trial)

  • Defining a content roadmap: Develop a strategic roadmap for the creation of further documentation and additional content types e.g., Tutorial videos (Guidde), Pendo walkthroughs etc. in alignment with product roadmap and customer needs.
  • High-level consultancy on customer education and customer marketing strategy.

Success Metrics

  • Qualitative feedback from early customers indicates resources are helpful, clear, and easy to use.
